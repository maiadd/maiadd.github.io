Maia Darmon

I'm an undergraduate student at McGill University, completing a degree in Honours Mathematics with a minor in Linguistics. My interests in math include group theory, representation theory, Galois theory, and algebraic topology. I am also exploring different areas of math and computer science, in particular logic, set theory and computability theory, and would like to expand my knowledge of theoretical computer science and fields which combine linguistics with math and computer science, such as semantics or computational linguistics.
